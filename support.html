Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress?

Delete and reinstall the app. All progress is stored locally on your device.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. Maths Gauntlet works entirely offline. All lessons, questions, and animations are included in the app.

What topics are covered?

The app covers 9 maths domains: Number, Algebra, Coordinate Geometry, Geometry, Mensuration, Trigonometry, Probability, Statistics, and Transformations & Vectors.

What is Core vs Extended tier?

Core covers the essential topics. Extended includes additional advanced content. You can switch between them in your Profile settings.
