Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 March 2026

Overview

Maths Gauntlet is a maths tutoring app that runs entirely on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

Maths Gauntlet does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your progress, settings, and learning data are stored locally on your device and are never sent to any server.

No Accounts Required

The app does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or date of birth.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies. No usage data is collected or shared with third parties.

No Internet Connection Required

Maths Gauntlet works fully offline. All content, including lessons, questions, and animations, is bundled within the app. No network requests are made.

Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any data whatsoever, the app is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Data Storage

Your learning progress (XP, streaks, question history) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is not accessible to us or any third party. Deleting the app removes all stored data.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices, we will update this page. Any future version that collects data will ask for your explicit consent first.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our support page.
